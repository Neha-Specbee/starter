Run a test using your actual customer data to understand what your match and fill rates are by each Insights Bundle.

## Getting Started

Have your sample file ready for upload? Let's get started!

Follow the step-by-step guide provided within platform seen in the screenshot below to upload your file:

<Frame>
![Match Test Steps](https://fullcontact-cx-static-assets.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/docs/Run_Match_Test_Platform.png)
</Frame>

Feel free to check out the [Person Insights Bundles](https://docs.fullcontact.com/docs/person-insights-bundles) while waiting for your file to finish uploading to review the available enrichment categories offered.

Once your file has finished uploading, you can now check out the test results under the **All Reports** tab.

The All Reports tab provides you with a high-level overview of all the match tests you've run.

<Frame>
![All Reports](https://fullcontact-cx-static-assets.s3.amazonaws.com/docs/34ASR9PEMk/CH8ABsto928M8g.png)
</Frame>

By clicking into a report from the All Reports tab, you can then drill down into specific match and fill rates across FullContact's Insight Bundles. Take a look at field-level results for age range, gender, estimated household income, and more by clicking on the eye ball icon next to each Insights Bundle.

<Frame>
![Results](https://fullcontact-cx-static-assets.s3.amazonaws.com/docs/34ASR9PEMk/94dQNx7Yn2HXGk.png)
</Frame>
